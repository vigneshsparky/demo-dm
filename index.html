Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

What is the duration of your courses?

+

Our courses range from 2 months to 6 months depending on the program. Full-time intensive programs are typically 3 months, while part-time programs may take 6 months.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

+

Yes, we have a dedicated career services team that helps with resume building, interview preparation, and connecting students with our network of employer partners.

What are the prerequisites for enrollment?

+

Most of our beginner courses require no prior experience. For advanced programs, we typically require basic knowledge in the field or completion of our introductory courses.

Can I pay in installments?

+

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. You can choose to pay in 2-4 installments depending on the program duration and cost.

Do you offer online learning options?

+

Yes, all our programs are available both in-person at our campuses and through our interactive online platform. You can choose the format that works best for you.
